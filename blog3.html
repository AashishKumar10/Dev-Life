My First Family Website –
Nain Family

This was my very first full HTML & CSS project, dedicated to my family. I created a responsive layout that includes individual pages for each member of my family — like my father, mother, sisters, and brother — but I kept the posts simple and just used names without labels.

The website includes a home page, a "Work" page showing everyone's roles, a "Blog" section, and even a footer with links and social media. I used custom fonts like 'Dancing Script' and 'Outfit' to make it visually appealing.

This project helped me understand layout techniques like Flexbox, custom fonts, and HTML linking between pages. It also gave me confidence to work on bigger projects.
