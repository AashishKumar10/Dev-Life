My Experience Learning MERN Stack
at Ducat

During my MERN Full Stack course at Ducat Institute, I got a deep understanding of modern web development. The stack — MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js — felt overwhelming at first, but with time and practice, I found how powerful and developer-friendly it is.

I started by learning React components, props, states, and lifecycle. Then moved on to backend with Node and Express where I built APIs and handled routing. MongoDB helped me understand NoSQL data management better.

This blog is a weekly log of my personal learning journey — from challenges with JavaScript to building a full CRUD app. It reflects how I gradually improved my confidence and started building full-fledged web apps.
